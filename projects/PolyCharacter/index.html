Implications


Aesthetics

I will use high quailty textures to supplement low quailty of models (poly character)
so I can make it more scary.
Also I will make it as detailed as I can to perform great quality.
Summary, I will create/use high quailty textures that can supplement low quailty of models.
In detail, I will add long hairs to the character that can cover whole body or at least their face.
This will make people to get immersed. Which can increase usability.

Functionality

I will put rigs as much as I can to make it able to walk with creepy postures.
This will include chasing animation, normal walking animations.
Also I will ensure my scary character works property with great animations.
Many amount of rigs and creepy postures will also make people to be immersed.

Usability

I will ensure that the outcome is fit to scary games that has dark theme.
Also I will add rigs on specific positions such as knee, elbow, and neck.
I will make the character easy to perform high quality animations.
This will be done by adding many rigs to the parts and spliting up body parts as much as I can.
The point of making character more creepy is aiming immerse people to the product that is using my outcome.

Accessibility

I will increase accessibility with a small amount of
body parts and being able to move as much body parts as possible.
By adding less rigs on less important parts such as fingers because
I won't use many finger movement.
