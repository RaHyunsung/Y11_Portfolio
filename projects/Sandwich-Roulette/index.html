Overview

The sandwich roulette is a turn based game,
basically randomb based game
(Well known example: Russian roulette) with sandwiches.
Two or more players can take turns
and choose one sandwich from a picnic basket.
Some of them is poisoned.

End Users

This game is targeted at gamblers, gamers, strategists for 13+ to 20 years old students in Western Springs College.

Requirements

Number Requirement Name Specific
1. Items
  • Poison detector
  • Heaing items (Poison cure)
  • Max inventory of four items (System)
2. Gambling
  • Random system for poisoned sandwiches
  • Strategies to win (Not 100% of luck)
  • Random items collecting system
3. Turn based system
  • A system that allows player to eat or pass when they get their turn if any players eats the poison they lose their health or if the sandwich was safe, they decide what to do with next sandwiches.
  • Rounds, turn based system (All health and items are replenished.)
  • Strategies
4. Win/Lose system
  • Damage system
  • Health bar
  • Heal system

Implications


Aesthetics

This game will be a pixel (dot) style game that is similar to the game called "Buckshot Roulette"
with a menu that is similar to the game called "GTA 5"

Ethical

Solution: Poisoned sandwich that can kill people, gambling with risk of death
Warn players with PG style button at the main menu
AND if they click it, show a contract sheet style warning sheet about violence.
